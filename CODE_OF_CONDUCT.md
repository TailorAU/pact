# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone contributing to the PACT specification.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Focus on the protocol and its technical merits
- Welcome newcomers and help them contribute
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately derailing discussions
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any related communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at [TailorAU](https://github.com/TailorAU). All reports will be reviewed and investigated.
